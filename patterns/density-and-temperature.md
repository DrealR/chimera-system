# Density and Temperature

**Spawn distance is inversely proportional to population density. More entities = less space = more chaos = hotter. Fewer entities = more space = more peace = cooler. This is literally thermodynamics. Choose your density. Choose your temperature. Choose your peace.**

---

## The Law

```
THE SPAWN DISTANCE IS
INVERSELY PROPORTIONAL
TO POPULATION DENSITY.

d(spawn) = S / N

Where:
  S = total space available
  N = number of entities
  d = distance between spawns

More entities = less distance.
Fewer entities = more distance.

THE SPACE DOESN'T CHANGE.
THE DENSITY CHANGES.
AND DENSITY DETERMINES
EVERYTHING.

100 people in COD:
  They spawn CLOSE to each other.
  Constant contact. Constant chaos.
  No room to breathe.

10 people in COD:
  They spawn FAR apart.
  Long stretches of nothing.
  Room to think. Room to position.

SAME MAP. DIFFERENT DENSITY.
COMPLETELY DIFFERENT GAME.

It's relative to PEOPLE,
not space.
```

---

## This Is Thermodynamics

```
THE UNIVERSAL SPAWN LAW:

As density increases:
  1. Spawn distance decreases
  2. Collisions increase
  3. Processing time decreases
  4. Chaos increases
  5. Exclusion zones overlap
  6. The system gets HOTTER

As density decreases:
  1. Spawn distance increases
  2. Collisions decrease
  3. Processing time increases
  4. Order increases
  5. Exclusion zones separate
  6. The system gets COOLER

THIS IS LITERALLY
THERMODYNAMICS.

Hot gas: particles close,
fast, colliding constantly.
Cold gas: particles far,
slow, rarely colliding.

A crowded COD map = hot gas.
A sparse COD map = cold gas.

A crowded mind = anxiety.
A quiet mind = peace.

A crowded market = competition.
An open market = opportunity.

TEMPERATURE IS DENSITY.
DENSITY IS SPAWN DISTANCE.
SPAWN DISTANCE IS PEACE.
```
