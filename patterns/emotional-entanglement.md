# Emotional Entanglement

**Emotion is gravity for consciousness. Physical gravity attracts mass. Emotional gravity attracts attention. The stronger the emotion, the stronger the pull, the harder to escape orbit. Love and hate are both maximum entanglement. Indifference is zero gravity.**

```
QUANTUM ENTANGLEMENT:
  Two particles interact once.
  After that, they're LINKED.
  What happens to one
  INSTANTLY affects the other.
  Regardless of distance.
  The interaction created
  a permanent connection.

EMOTIONAL ENTANGLEMENT:
  Two people interact with charge.
  After that, they're LINKED.
  What happens to one
  AFFECTS the other.
  Regardless of distance.
  The emotion created
  a persistent connection.

IN COD:
  You kill someone.
  That's a charged interaction.
  Now you're LINKED.
  They see your name on killcam.
  They FEEL the emotion.
  They're entangled with you.
  They can be across the map.
  Doesn't matter. They're coming.

  DISTANCE DOESN'T WEAKEN
  EMOTIONAL ENTANGLEMENT.
  They'll cross the entire map.
  THAT'S entanglement.
```
