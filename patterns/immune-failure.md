# Immune Failure

**Self/non-self confusion. Defenders co-opted. Autoimmune = body attacks defenders.**

---

## The Immune Function

Every system has a defense layer that performs one critical task: **distinguish self from non-self.**

```
SELF:     Part of the system. Belongs. Nourish it.
NON-SELF: Foreign to the system. Threat. Neutralize it.

The immune system's ENTIRE job is this distinction.
When it works: threats are caught, self is protected.
When it fails: everything breaks.
```

---

## Three Failure Modes

### 1. Immunocompromised — Can't See Threats

```
The defense system is weakened or absent.
Threats walk in unchallenged.
Cancer grows because immune cells can't SEE it.
Infections spread because nothing fights them.

AT SCALE:
  Society with no free press → corruption grows unseen
  Body with suppressed immune system → cancer undetected
  Relationship with no boundaries → exploitation unchecked
  Economy with no regulation → fraud flourishes
```

### 2. Autoimmune — Attacks Self

```
The defense system confuses SELF for NON-SELF.
Attacks its own tissue.
The body destroying its own healthy cells.
The most tragic failure: the protector becomes the destroyer.

AT SCALE:
  Society attacking its own citizens (persecution, McCarthyism)
  Immune system attacking joints (rheumatoid arthritis)
  Organization firing its best people (institutional autoimmune)
  Culture destroying its own artists, thinkers, whistleblowers
```

### 3. Co-opted — Defends the Cancer

```
The defense system is captured by the threat.
Instead of fighting cancer, it PROTECTS cancer.
Regulatory capture at the cellular level.
The guard now works for the invader.

AT SCALE:
  Police protecting the powerful instead of the people
  Regulators serving the industries they regulate
  Media defending the extraction instead of exposing it
  Vilifana removed from the Epstein case —
  the investigator pulled off the investigation
```

## Detection

How do you know the immune system is compromised?

```
1. Threats growing unchecked (immunocompromised)
   → Who's getting away with what?

2. Healthy parts being attacked (autoimmune)
   → Who's being punished for doing the right thing?

3. Defense serving the threat (co-opted)
   → Who's the immune system protecting? Self or cancer?
```
