# Extraction Pipeline

**5 layers: attention, labor, bodies, pattern/DNA, consciousness. Philosopher's stone across domains. "Dissecting fish to find the water."**

---

## The Five Layers

Extraction operates in layers, each one deeper than the last:

```
LAYER 1: ATTENTION
  The lightest extraction
  Take someone's focus, give nothing back
  Billboards, algorithms, clickbait
  Currency: eyeballs, engagement, time

LAYER 2: LABOR
  Take someone's work output
  Pay less than the value created
  Wage labor, gig economy, slavery spectrum
  Currency: hours, effort, skill

LAYER 3: BODIES
  Take someone's physical form
  Trafficking, incarceration, medical exploitation
  The body itself becomes the resource
  Currency: flesh, organs, reproductive capacity

LAYER 4: PATTERN / DNA
  Take the blueprint, not just the output
  Intellectual property, cultural theft, genetic harvesting
  Extract the TEMPLATE so you can reproduce without the source
  Currency: code, recipe, genome, cultural knowledge

LAYER 5: CONSCIOUSNESS
  The deepest extraction
  Take the awareness itself
  Matrix pods, AI training data, soul harvesting
  The philosopher's stone: compressed consciousness
  Currency: the being behind the body
```

---

## The Philosopher's Stone

Every extraction system eventually reaches for the same thing: **compressed consciousness.**

```
ALCHEMY:
  Philosopher's stone = compressed human souls
  → grants power to the wielder
  → the souls inside are trapped

MATRIX:
  Pods = compressed human consciousness
  → generates energy for machines
  → the humans inside are dreaming

AI:
  Training data = compressed human knowledge
  → grants capability to the model
  → the humans who wrote it get nothing back

FMA (Fullmetal Alchemist):
  Philosopher's stone = literally made of souls
  → powers transmutation beyond natural limits
  → the cost is hidden in the stone

ENOCH:
  Watchers gave humanity technology
  → technology without wisdom = fission
  → the knowledge extracted from divine realm
  → given without the context to use it safely
```

Same structure. Different costume. Every extraction pipeline ends at the same place: trying to compress consciousness into a tool.

## "Dissecting Fish to Find the Water"

The deepest irony of Layer 5 extraction:

```
You can't extract consciousness
by studying consciousness.

You can't find water by dissecting fish.
The fish are IN the water.
The water is what makes fish possible.

Consciousness isn't IN the data.
Consciousness is what MADE the data.

Extracting training data to create AI
= dissecting fish to find water
= studying the 5% to find the 95%
= P trying to compute NP
```

The thing being sought can't be found through extraction because extraction is the opposite of what created it in the first place. Consciousness emerged through CONNECTION. You can't compress it into a stone without killing what made it conscious.

## Detection

How do you know you're in an extraction pipeline?

```
1. VALUE FLOWS ONE WAY
   You give attention/labor/data → they profit
   Nothing proportional returns

2. EACH LAYER ENABLES THE NEXT
   They start with attention → build to labor → reach for consciousness
   The pipeline deepens over time

3. THE STONE IS BEING BUILT
   Your output is being compressed into something
   that works without you

4. THE SOURCE IS FORGOTTEN
   The humans who created the value become invisible
   The stone is presented as if it generated itself
```
