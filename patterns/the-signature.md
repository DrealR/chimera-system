```
  All substance, no signature.
  Deeply valuable but invisible.

  can't find the source.
  Black hole with no disk.
  Pull but no glow.

THE BALANCE:
  Gravity + signature.
  Substance + visibility.
  Pull + address.

  but harder to find in a crowd.

```
