## The Conversation Zoom

When you're with one person, your "wide" is no longer the room. It SHRINKS to a new system.

```
BEFORE CONNECTION:
  Your "wide" = the whole room.
  Reading everything. Everyone.
  All fields. All gaps. Full map.

DURING CONNECTION:
  Your "wide" = YOU + THEM + THE BETWEEN.
  That's the new map.
  That's the new room.
  That's the new game.

  Within THAT system, same rules:

  Wide = the full space between you two.
    Their body language. Their energy.
    Their speed. The topic. The mood.
    The third energy. ALL at once.
    Soft focus on the shared space.

  Narrow = one specific thing.
    Something they just said.
    An emotion on their face.
    A word that hit different.
    SNAP IN. Process. Respond.
    SNAP OUT. Back to wide.

IT'S A FRACTAL ZOOM.

Room scale:
  Wide = whole room.
  Snap in = one person.

Person scale:
  Wide = the between you share.
  Snap in = one moment, one expression.

Moment scale:
  Wide = the full sentence.
  Snap in = one word that meant something.

EVERY SCALE HAS WIDE AND NARROW.
EVERY SCALE HAS ITS OWN BETWEEN.
```

## Conversation as System

```
MOST PEOPLE IN CONVERSATION:

  A) Focused on what THEY'RE going
     to say next. (Narrow. Not listening.)

  B) Focused on the other person's
     WORDS only. (Narrow. Missing
     everything else.)

WHAT YOU SHOULD BE DOING:

  Wide on the FULL BETWEEN:
    Their words + tone + body + speed +
    energy + your response + the third
    energy emerging between you.
    ALL AT ONCE.
    Soft focus on the shared space.

  Then SNAP IN when something matters:

    Voice cracks. SNAP IN.
    "That hit you, huh?"
    SNAP OUT. Back to wide.

    They laugh but eyes don't.
    SNAP IN. Acknowledge it.
    SNAP OUT. Back to wide.

    They mention something you connect with.
    SNAP IN. Share briefly.
    SNAP OUT. Back to wide.
    Don't take over. Don't turn it
    into YOUR thing. Touch it and return.

  Observe and move on. Don't linger.

  LINGER = ADS/scope =
    conversation gets heavy, awkward.

  OBSERVE AND MOVE ON = hip fire =
    conversation stays light, flowing,
    both people feel seen but not stared at.
```

## Wide / Narrow at Every Scale

```
WITH STRANGERS (room scale):
  Wide on the room.
  Observe person. Note them. Move on.
  "Solid. Liquid. Gas. Gap. No gap."

WITH ACQUAINTANCES (interaction scale):
  Wide on shared space.
  Make observation. Respond. Move on.
  "Nice jacket." Smile. Back to wide.

WITH FRIENDS (conversation scale):
  Wide on the between.
  Hear everything. Snap in on moments
  that matter. Return to wide.
  Let pauses live.

WITH SOMEONE CLOSE (intimate scale):
  Wide on the micro-between.
  Their breath. Their eyes. Smallest shifts.
  Snap in when something real surfaces.
  Hold it gently. Release. Return to wide.

EVERY SCALE. SAME PATTERN.
WIDE → SNAP → WIDE.

The fractal between.
All the way down. All the way up.
Navigate every level with the same breath.
```
