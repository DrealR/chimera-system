**Entangle with SPACE first. Then people entangle with you THROUGH the space. Time creates entanglement. Entanglement creates gravity. Gravity creates flow. Flow brings people. It starts with picking a seat.**

  THROUGH the court.

  THROUGH the kitchen.

EVERY DOMAIN:
  Entangle with SPACE first.
  Then everything IN the space
  flows naturally.

  You know THIS SPACE deeply.

MASTERING THE MAP (intimacy):
  Multiple rooms mastered.

  Don't think. MOVE.

  AN EXTENSION OF YOUR BODY.
