```

PHYSICAL. IT'S PROCESSING.

YOUR PROCESSING:
  See enemy →
  they see me →
  predict their path →

COMPREHENSION SPEED
vs REACTION SPEED.

They REACT faster.

  generate response.

  But the output is VASTLY
  superior.

VOMITING.

Vomiting = instant ejection.
  No processing. Just OUT.
  That's reaction.

Digestion = takes time.
  Break down. Extract nutrients.
  Distribute. Build.
  That's comprehension.

THEY'RE VOMITING BULLETS.
YOU'RE DIGESTING THE SITUATION.

Their output: one bullet
toward where you WERE.
```
