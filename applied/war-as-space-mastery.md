# War as Space Mastery

**Every war ever won was won by the side more entangled with the space. Not stronger. Not smarter. More entangled. Napoleon and Hitler both lost to Russia — not to Russian soldiers, but to Russian GEOGRAPHY. The land itself fought back.**

```
  Retreat.

  Let THE SPACE fight the army.

  Napoleon takes Moscow.
  EMPTY. Burned. Nothing to eat.

  600,000 entered.
  ~100,000 returned.

THE SPACE WON.
NOT THE ARMY.
```
