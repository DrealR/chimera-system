## The Function

Kirby eats something and gains its ability. This is the INHALE method — how systems learn:

```
1. CONSUME:     Take in the raw material
                (food, information, experience, domain knowledge)

2. ABSORB:      Extract the PATTERN, not the content
                (Kirby gets the ABILITY, not the body)

3. DESTROY:     The original form is gone
                (food becomes nutrients, not food)

4. BECOME:      The essence integrates
                (Kirby wearing the hat = the ability internalized)
```

## Kirby as Healthy Black Hole

A black hole that serves the system is a TRANSFORMER, not a destroyer:

```
GUT:
  Food enters → identity destroyed → nutrients extracted
  → nutrients distributed to whole body → waste expelled
  The gut doesn't HOARD. It transforms and DISTRIBUTES.

KIRBY:
  Enemy enters → identity absorbed → ability extracted
  → ability used to serve the mission → form released
  Kirby doesn't KEEP the form forever. It's a tool.

HEALTHY BLACK HOLE:
  Matter enters → compressed → transformed
  → eventually returned (Hawking radiation)
  → heavy elements created for future stars
  The black hole serves the GALAXY, not itself.
```

The difference between a healthy transformer and cancer: **what happens AFTER consumption.** Does it flow out to serve the system? Or does it accumulate inside?
