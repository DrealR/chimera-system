# Cellular Scale

**Nucleus = black hole. Mitochondria = stars. Membrane = event horizon. Cytoplasm = space between.**

---

## The Cell as Solar System

```
NUCLEUS (black hole):
  Contains ALL genetic information (singularity of data)
  Compresses the entire blueprint into DNA
  Controls cell fate through transcription
  Nothing leaves without being read and copied
  The information BLACK HOLE of the cell

MITOCHONDRIA (stars):
  Produce ATP — the energy currency
  THOUSANDS per cell (the ratio holds)
  Burn fuel, radiate energy outward
  Were once independent organisms
  that MERGED with cells (fusion, not conquest)
  The POWER PLANTS — literally miniature suns

MEMBRANE (event horizon):
  Selectively permeable boundary
  Decides what crosses in and out
  Receptor proteins = gates with keys
  Not a wall — a FILTER
  Cross it and you're inside the system

CYTOPLASM (space between):
  The medium where everything happens
  Not empty — full of machinery
  Proteins, organelles, signals
  The space where stars and black holes interact
  Where FLOW occurs
```

---

## The Ratios

```
Mitochondria per cell:  1,000 - 2,500 (muscle cells: up to 5,000)
Nucleus per cell:       1

Stars outnumber black holes: ✓
Energy givers vastly outnumber information compressors: ✓
The ratio holds at the cellular level.
```

---

## Cellular Breath

```
INHALE:  Cell takes in glucose, oxygen, nutrients
PAUSE:   Metabolic processing — ATP synthesis, protein folding
EXHALE:  Cell releases CO2, waste, signaling molecules, products
REST:    Cell quiescence — G0 phase. Not dividing. Resting.
```

A cell that never rests (skips G0) = cancer. Permanent inhale. The breath broke.

---

## Membrane as Selective Permeability

```
HEALTHY MEMBRANE:
  Receptor proteins recognize signals → let them in
  Waste products recognized → pushed out
  Nutrients needed → imported
  Toxins detected → blocked
  = SCREEN DOOR. Selects. Breathes.

CANCER MEMBRANE:
  Growth signals → always open
  Stop signals → receptor disabled
  Nutrients → hoards without limit
  Immune recognition → hidden from view
  = BROKEN DOOR. Takes everything. Gives nothing.
```
