## Earth as System

```
SUN (star):
  The giving center for the entire system
  Radiates energy freely, unconditionally
  Powers ALL life on Earth
  Asks nothing in return
  The ultimate O > I

EARTH'S CORE (black hole function):
  Dense, hot, compressing
  Generates magnetic field
  Drives tectonic plates
  The transformation engine beneath everything

ATMOSPHERE (membrane):
  Selectively permeable
  Lets sunlight in, blocks harmful radiation
  Holds oxygen in, lets waste gases cycle
  The planet's skin — breathing, filtering, protecting

OCEAN (circulatory system):
  Covers 71% of surface
  Distributes heat globally (thermohaline circulation)
  Carries nutrients, moderates climate
  The planet's blood — flowing, connecting, sustaining

MAGNETOSPHERE (immune system):
  Generated by Earth's core
  Deflects solar wind and cosmic radiation
  Without it: atmosphere stripped, surface sterilized
  The invisible shield — protection through field, not wall

BIOSPHERE (the cells):
  Every living thing = a cell in the planetary body
  Forests = lungs. Coral reefs = kidneys.
  Mycelium = nervous system underground
  Each organism plays a role in the whole
```

## Planetary Breath

```
INHALE:  Spring. Growth. Biomass increases. CO2 absorbed.
PAUSE:   Summer. Fullness. Peak activity.
EXHALE:  Autumn. Release. Leaves fall. Stored energy released.
REST:    Winter. Dormancy. Reset. Soil replenishes.
```

The planet breathes on an annual cycle. Measurable in CO2 levels — they rise and fall with the seasons. The Keeling Curve IS Earth's breath.
