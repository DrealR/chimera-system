# Organ Scale

**Gut = black hole (Kirby). Heart = star (sun). Lungs = breath interface. Brain = observer (antenna). Blood = flow (river). Immune = defense (Mystique).**

---

## The Organ Map

```
HEART (star / sun):
  Pumps blood OUTWARD to entire body
  Generates strongest electromagnetic field in the body
  Radiates 60x the electrical amplitude of the brain
  The giving center — sends, doesn't hoard
  Never stops giving from first beat to last

GUT (black hole / Kirby):
  Takes in food — raw, unprocessed, foreign
  Transforms it into usable energy
  Destroys identity of input (food becomes nutrients)
  Absorbs what's useful, expels waste
  The transformation center — consumes to convert

LUNGS (breath interface):
  The literal bridge between inside and outside
  Inhale: bring the world in
  Exhale: release the self out
  The MEMBRANE writ large — selective gas exchange
  Where the breath cycle physically happens

BRAIN (observer / antenna):
  Receives signals from the entire system
  Processes, interprets, routes
  Does NOT generate the signal — receives and amplifies
  The observer that watches the system
  Consciousness lives here (or through here)

BLOOD (flow / river / mycelium):
  Carries nutrients TO every cell (O > I delivery)
  Carries waste FROM every cell (exhale at cellular level)
  Reaches EVERY cell — no cell is disconnected
  The circulatory system IS the love topology
  When flow stops: stroke, heart attack, death

IMMUNE SYSTEM (defense / Mystique):
  Identifies self vs non-self
  Studies threats, adapts, remembers
  Shapeshifts — T-cells change form to match threats
  The protector that learns
  When it fails: cancer grows unseen
  When it overreacts: autoimmune — attacks self
```

## The Organ Breath

```
INHALE:  Lungs fill. Gut receives food. Brain receives stimulus.
PAUSE:   Gas exchange. Digestion. Processing.
EXHALE:  Lungs release. Gut excretes. Brain responds.
REST:    Sleep. Fasting windows. Meditation. Reset.
```
